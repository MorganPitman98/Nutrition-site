Macronutrients

Carbohydrates

Provide energy for your body. Found in fruits, vegetables, grains, and legumes.

Proteins

Essential for muscle repair and growth. Sources include meat, fish, eggs, dairy, and legumes.

Fats

Support cell growth and hormone production. Found in oils, nuts, seeds, and fish.

Micronutrients

Vitamins

Support immune function, bone health, and energy production. Get them from fruits and vegetables.

Minerals

Help with hydration, muscle function, and more. Common sources include dairy, meat, and leafy greens.
